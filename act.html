Student Activities

Club: @Connect

Fostering Innovation, One Connection at a Time

@Connect is the official student-run technical club for the Department of IoT. Our mission is to create a vibrant community of IoT enthusiasts, tinkerers, and builders. We bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world application by providing a platform for students to collaborate, learn, and innovate.

Throughout the year, @Connect organizes a variety of events, including:

  • Hands-on workshops on new sensors and platforms.
  • Guest lectures from industry experts.
  • Internal hackathons and ideation challenges.
  • Collaborative group projects for social impact.

Tech Fest: ASTRA

The Future of Tech, Unleashed

ASTRA is the annual technical fest of the Department of Internet of Things, a two-day extravaganza that brings together bright minds from across the university and beyond. It's a celebration of technology, creativity, and problem-solving, designed to challenge students and showcase their talents.

Our flagship events typically include:

  • A 24-Hour IoT Hackathon (Solve-a-Thon).
  • Technical Paper Presentation contests.
  • Robo-Wars and Drone Racing events.
  • Project expos and technical quizzes with exciting prizes.
