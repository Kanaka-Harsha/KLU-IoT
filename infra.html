Infrastructure

About Our Infrastructure

Our B.Tech in IoT is a comprehensive 4-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with the multidisciplinary knowledge required to excel in the rapidly evolving field of IoT. The program blends core concepts from electronics, computer science, and data communication, preparing students to design, build, and manage connected systems.

Laboratories & Facilities

The department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories to provide practical, hands-on learning, ensuring students can translate theory into practice.

IoT Innovation Lab

Equipped with various development boards, sensors, and actuators for rapid prototyping of IoT solutions.

Embedded Systems Lab

Focuses on microcontroller programming, interfacing, and real-time operating systems (RTOS).

Communication & Networking Lab

Tools for simulating and analyzing network protocols and wireless communication technologies.

Cloud & Data Analytics Lab

High-performance workstations for working with cloud platforms and running data-intensive applications.

Components Available

The department houses a wide range of advanced electronic components and modules, enabling students to design, prototype, and test real-world IoT systems with precision and creativity.
