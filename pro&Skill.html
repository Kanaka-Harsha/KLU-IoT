Projects & Skilling

Our Projects

Our B.Tech in IoT is a comprehensive 4-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with the multidisciplinary knowledge required to excel in the rapidly evolving field of IoT. The program blends core concepts from electronics, computer science, and data communication, preparing students to design, build, and manage connected systems.

Laboratories & Facilities

The department boasts state-of-the-art laboratories to provide practical, hands-on learning, ensuring students can translate theory into practice.

IoT Innovation Lab

Equipped with various development boards (like Raspberry Pi, Arduino, ESP32), a wide array of sensors (temperature, humidity, motion, etc.), and actuators for rapid prototyping of end-to-end IoT solutions.

Embedded Systems Lab

Focuses on microcontroller programming (ARM, AVR), interfacing peripherals, and understanding real-time operating systems (RTOS). Students learn to build reliable, low-level device software.

Communication & Networking Lab

Tools and hardware (like routers, switches, and simulation software like NS3/GNS3) for simulating, analyzing, and configuring network protocols (TCP/IP, MQTT) and wireless communication technologies (Wi-Fi, LoRaWAN, Bluetooth).

Cloud & Data Analytics Lab

High-performance workstations equipped for working with major cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP). Students run data-intensive applications, train ML models, and manage data pipelines for IoT analytics.

Components Available

The department houses a wide range of advanced electronic components and modules, enabling students to design, prototype, and test real-world IoT systems with precision and creativity.
