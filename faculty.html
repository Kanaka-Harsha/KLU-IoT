Our Esteemed Academics

The faculty of the Department of Internet of Things (IoT) are a diverse group of distinguished academics, researchers, and industry experts. With deep knowledge spanning from embedded systems and sensor technology to cloud computing and AI, our team is dedicated to pioneering research and providing an exceptional, hands-on education. They are mentors committed to guiding the next generation of IoT innovators and leaders.
