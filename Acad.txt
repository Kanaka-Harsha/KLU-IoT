    <section id="academic-content">
        
        <div class="acad-section">
            <h2>B.Tech in Internet of Things (IoT)</h2>
            <p>Our B.Tech in IoT is a comprehensive 4-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with the multidisciplinary knowledge required to excel in the rapidly evolving field of IoT. The program blends core concepts from electronics, computer science, and data communication, preparing students to design, build, and manage connected systems.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="acad-section">
            <h2>Curriculum Highlights</h2>
            <p>The curriculum is meticulously crafted to cover all aspects of the IoT ecosystem. Key areas of study include:</p>
            <ul>
                <li>Sensor Technology and Actuators</li>
                <li>Embedded Systems and Microcontrollers (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP32)</li>
                <li>Wireless Communication and Networking (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa, 5G)</li>
                <li>IoT Protocols (MQTT, CoAP, HTTP)</li>
                <li>Cloud Computing and IoT Platforms (AWS IoT, Azure IoT, Google Cloud IoT)</li>
                <li>Data Analytics and Machine Learning for IoT</li>
                <li>IoT Security and Privacy</li>
                <li>Application Development (Web and Mobile)</li>
            </ul>
        </div>

        <div class="acad-section">
            <h2>Program Structure</h2>
            <p>The program follows a semester-based credit system, offering a balanced mix of theoretical and practical learning. The curriculum includes:</p>
            <ul>
                <li><b>Foundation Courses:</b> Mathematics, Physics, Basic Electrical and Electronics Engineering.</li>
                <li><b>Core Courses:</b> In-depth subjects directly related to IoT hardware, software, and networking.</li>
                <li><b>Elective Courses:</b> Specialized topics allowing students to tailor their learning path (e.g., Smart Cities, Industrial IoT, Connected Healthcare, Smart Agriculture).</li>
                <li><b>Project Work:</b> Hands-on mini-projects, a year-long capstone project, and opportunities for research publications.</li>
                <li><b>Internships:</b> Mandatory industry internships to gain invaluable real-world experience and build professional connections.</li>
            </ul>
        </div>
