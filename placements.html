Our Placement & Training Process

We provide comprehensive, end-to-end support to ensure our students are fully prepared for the demands of the tech industry. Our process is a core part of the curriculum, not an afterthought.

  • Foundation Skilling: Dedicated courses in data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving right from the early semesters.
  • Industry Certifications: Integrated training for high-demand certifications from AWS, Cisco, and Taras.
  • Resume Workshops: One-on-one sessions with faculty to build professional resumes that highlight key projects and skills.
  • Mock Interviews: Rigorous mock technical and HR interviews conducted by industry experts and alumni.
  • Company-Specific Training: Tailored bootcamps and workshops focused on the specific hiring patterns of top recruiters.
